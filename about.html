A Short Intro

I am a Bangladeshi aspiring young developer. At present, I am an attendee of B.Sc. (Hons) program at Leading University. I like to read mostly novels and poems. I love listening songs of rock genre.

Family History and Early Life

I (SMBOND) was born on July 27, 2003 in SYLHET, BANGLADESH, to MD Sultan Mahmood, a retired govt officer and Fatema Jahan. I am the youngest of three children, with a brother and a sister. My family had a significant position in the parliament of the then Pakistan and early Bangladesh. My grandfather was a freedom fighter during the Liberation War of Bangladesh and was in charge of the mechanized infantry division of greater Sylhet. But due to an unprecedented incident, he was arrested and taken by the Pakistan army before he could join his forces. He died in 2010 and was given the prestigious guard of honor by the police and the military. His younger brother however, Dr. Khasruzzaman Chowdhury had finished his Phd. in Economics from the Syracuse University and MPA from the Harvard University in the United States. He was among the few Bengalees to get in the CSP in 1967 and was in the top 10% of whole the then Pakistan. He served as the Assistant Commisioner in Dhaka from 1969 to 1970 and was appointed as the After the war broke out, he joined the war as a freedom fighter. He successfully led a team of fighters and managed to gain control of a certain area in the country. My father completed his B.A from the Chattogram University in 1986 and joined the Bangladesh Customs during the 90s after passing his BCS examination. During my childhood, I grew up in a small locality, in the heart of Sylhet town. It was an area of peace and tranquility and the people in the whole locale was like a huge family. I passed my days mostly playing in the locale during the day after coming home from school. I made a bunch of friends there with whom I share wonderful memories.

Education

I started my school life when I was 6. I went to a small school in town called Pioneer School and College up untill class 4. In class 4, I joined Scholarshome and completed my grades from there for long 10 years. I passed my high school from there too. Now I am currently attending Leading University for my B.Sc. in CSE program.
